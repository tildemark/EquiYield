## Overview

EquiYield is provided free-of-charge under the EquiYield Free Software License for personal, educational, and non-commercial use. However, certain activities require a **Commercial License Agreement**.

## When You Need a Commercial License

A Commercial License is required for:

### 1. **Software Modifications & Development**
   - Custom enhancements or feature additions
   - Derivative works or forks for commercial purposes
   - Integration modules or extensions sold separately
   - White-label or private-labeled versions

### 2. **Professional Services**
   - Implementation and deployment services (for-profit)
   - Consulting and advisory services
   - System integration and customization
   - Training and certification programs (paid)
   - Managed services or support contracts

### 3. **Commercial Use Cases**
   - Bundling with commercial products or services
   - SaaS or cloud-based offerings
   - Resale or redistribution with modifications
   - Use in for-profit business operations requiring modifications

### 4. **Other Restricted Activities**
   - Remove or modify copyright notices
   - Create competing products based on the Software
   - Offer hosted versions with premium features
